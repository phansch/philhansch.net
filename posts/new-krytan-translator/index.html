New Krytan Translator

— 2012-07-14 , 1 min read

Here's what I've been working on, the past couple of days.

http://tools.plyturon.net/translator/

Update: Unfortunately the translator is not up anymore as I retired the project. If you are interested, you can drop me a mail at: hi AT phansch.net.

This tool translates any text to New Krytan, the language used by the inhabitants of Kryta in Guild Wars 2. The language is based on a very simple substitution cipher. Additionally there is a decimal numeral system in place.

Go check it out!
